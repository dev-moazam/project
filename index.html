HOW TO PLAY?

Step #1:

Download altV Client

Begin by downloading the altV client, the platform that powers vPursuit. You can find the client on the official altV website. Follow the download instructions provided there to ensure a smooth installation process.

Step #2:

Connect to vPursuit

After successfully installing the altV client, launch it. Use Direct Connect: Enter "join.vpursuit.com" in the direct connect field. This will directly connect you to the vPursuit server..

Step #3:

Register your account

Once connected to vPursuit, you need to register an account to personalize your gaming experience. Click on Register option and fill your informations.

Step #4:

Enjoy

With your account set up, you're ready to immerse yourself in vPursuit. Explore the diverse array of vehicles, and collaborate with fellow players through the clan system. Have fun navigating the dynamic gameplay.

* If you encounter any challenges with the connection process, we encourage you to seek assistance by joining our official Discord server for prompt and expert support.
